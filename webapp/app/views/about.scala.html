What is this?

There is mounting evidence that the volume of information being consumed by the average user is increasing. The growth in content consumption aids doomscrooling. The result is a population that is increasingly facing the negative effects of this phenomena in their lives as argued by this, this and this.

NewsSnips is way to stay up-to-date on the trends of the news cycle without getting sucked into it. Along with machine learning, the platform aggregates the content from the sources into metrics that are indicative of trends.
